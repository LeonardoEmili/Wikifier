   A production team is the group of technical staff who produce a play,  |television_show|Television_Show| ,  |recording|Sound_Recording_And_Reproduction| , or film. Generally the term refers to all individuals responsible for the technical aspects of creating of a particular product, regardless of where in the process their expertize is required, or how long they are involved in the project. For example, in a theatrical performance, the production team includes not only the  |running_crew|Running_Crew| , but also the  |theatrical_producer|Theatrical_Producer| ,  |designers|Designer|  and  |theatre_direction|Theatre_Direction| .     A  |production_company|Production_Company|  in  |filmmaking|Filmmaking|  is composed of a  |film_crew|Film_Crew|  and a  |television_crew|Television_Crew|  in  |video_production|Video_Production| .     In music, the term production team typically refers to a group of individuals filling the role of  |record_producer|Record_Producer|  usually reserved for one individual. Some examples of musical production teams include  |Matmos|Matmos|  and  |D-Influence|D-Influence| .
