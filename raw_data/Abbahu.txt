His  |rabbinic|Rabbinic|  education was acquired mainly at  |Tiberias|Tiberias| , in the academy presided over by  |R._Johanan|R._Johanan| , with whom his relations were almost those of a son. He frequently made pilgrimages to Tiberias, even after he had become well known as  |rector|Rector|  of the Caesarean academy. Yerushalmi Shabbat  8:1, page 11a in Bombergs Venice edition , 54b in current editions ; Yerushalmi  Pesahim  10:1, page 37c in Bombergs Venice edition .     Abbahu was an authority on weights and measures.  Yerushalmi Terumot  5:3 page 43c in Bombergs Venice edition .
