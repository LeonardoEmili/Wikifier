        The  |Netherlands_Antilles|Netherlands_Antilles|  was a  |constituent_country|Countries_Of_The_Kingdom_Of_The_Netherlands|  in the  |Caribbean_Sea|Caribbean_Sea| . It consisted of two  |island_groups|Island_Group| , the  |ABC_islands|Abc_Islands| |_Curaçao|Curaçao| ,  |Bonaire|Bonaire|  and  |Aruba|Aruba|  just north of Venezuela, and the  |SSS_islands|Sss_Islands|  east of the  |Virgin_Islands|Virgin_Islands| .     The Netherlands Antilles had 960 km² of land, which included no major lakes or other bodies of water.     Territory included the islands of  |Aruba|Aruba|  until 1986 and  |Curaçao|Curaçao| ,  |Bonaire|Bonaire| ,  |Saba|Saba| ,  |Sint_Eustatius|Sint_Eustatius| ,  |Sint_Maarten|Sint_Maarten|   until 2010 when the Netherlands Antilles was  |dissolved|Dissolution_Of_The_Netherlands_Antilles| . Its only land boundary was with  |France|France|  on the island of Saint Martin, which was 10.2 kilometers in length. The Netherlands Antilles had 364 kilometers of coastline.
