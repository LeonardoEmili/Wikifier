     The laws of infernal dynamics are an  |adage|Adage|  about the cursedness of the  |universe|Universe| . Attributed to Science fiction author  |David_Gerrold|David_Gerrold| , the laws are as follows:     An object in motion will be moving in the wrong direction.   An object at rest will be in the wrong place.   The energy required to move an object in the correct direction, or put it in the right place, will be more than you wish to expend but not so much as to make the task impossible.     The laws are a parody on the first and second of  |Newtons_laws_of_motion|Newtons_Laws_Of_Motion|  in the spirit of  |Murphys_law|Murphys_Law| .
