It claims an  |exclusive_economic_zone|Exclusive_Economic_Zone|  of 200  nm, and a  |territorial_sea|Territorial_Sea|  of 12  nm. It is one of worlds largest  |coral_islands|Coral_Island| .       Niues climate is  |tropical|Tropical| , modified by south-east  |trade_winds|Trade_Winds| .  |Cyclones|Cyclones|  pose a natural hazard.          The terrain consists of steep coastal  |cliffs|Cliff|  made from  |limestone|Limestone|  and a central  |plateau|Plateau| . The lowest point is at sea level, and the highest is an unnamed point near  |Mutalau|Mutalau|  settlement, at 68 m.
