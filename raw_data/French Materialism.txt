French materialism is the name given to a handful of  |French|France|  18th-century philosophers during the  |Age_of_Enlightenment|Age_Of_Enlightenment| , many of them clustered around the  |salon|Salon|  of  |Baron_dHolbach|Baron_Dholbach| . Although there are important differences between them, all of them were  |materialists|Materialism|  who believed that the world was made up of a single substance, matter, the motions and properties of which could be used to explain all phenomena.
