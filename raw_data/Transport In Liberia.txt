   Transport in Liberia consist of railways, highways, seaports and airports.          Historically, three railways were built in Liberia to export ore from mines; they were damaged during civil war, and as of August 2010 only one had been brought back into service.     There are no rail connections with other countries, although there has been a proposal to extend the Bong mine railway to serve a mine across the border in  |Guinea|Transport_In_Guinea| .
