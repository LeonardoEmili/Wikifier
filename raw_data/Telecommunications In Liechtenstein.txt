This article concerns the systems of communications in Liechtenstein.  |Liechtenstein|Liechtenstein|  possesses a number of modern communications systems, some of which are shared with the neighbouring country of  |Switzerland|Switzerland| . The country code and top-level domain for Liechtenstein is  |.li|.Li| .       Liechtensteins automatic telephone system is connected to the  |Swiss|Communications_In_Switzerland|  telephone networks via cable and microwave radio relay systems. There are 20,072 main line telephones in the country served by the network. In April 1999, Liechtenstein ceased to be a part of the  |Swiss_telephone_numbering_plan|Telephone_Numbers_In_Switzerland| , in which the country used the Swiss area code 075 , opting instead to have a unique  |country_calling_code|Country_Calling_Code| ,  |+423|+423|  . There are 44  |internet_service_providers|Internet_Service_Providers|  in Liechtenstein and Switzerland combined.       There are four  |FM|Fm_Broadcasting|  radio broadcast stations in Liechtenstein serving 21,000  |radios|Radio| . The television broadcast system is linked to the  |Swiss_networks|Television_In_Switzerland|.
