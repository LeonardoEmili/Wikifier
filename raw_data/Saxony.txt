           Saxony , officially the Free State of Saxony , is a landlocked federal  |state|States_Of_Germany|  of  |Germany|Germany| , bordering the federal states of  |Brandenburg|Brandenburg| ,  |Saxony_Anhalt|Saxony_Anhalt| ,  |Thuringia|Thuringia| , and  |Bavaria|Bavaria| , as well as the  |countries|Countries|  of  |Poland|Poland|   and the  |Czech_Republic|Czech_Republic|  . Its capital is  |Dresden|Dresden| , and its largest city is  |Leipzig|Leipzig| .     Saxony is the tenth largest of Germanys sixteen states, with an area of , and the sixth most populous, with 4 million people.     The history of the state of Saxony spans more than a millennium. It has been a medieval  |duchy|Duchy_Of_Saxony| , an  |electorate|Electorate_Of_Saxony|  of the  |Holy_Roman_Empire|Holy_Roman_Empire| , a  |kingdom|Kingdom_Of_Saxony| , and twice a republic.     The area of the modern state of Saxony should not be confused with  |Old_Saxony|Old_Saxony| , the area inhabited by  |Saxons|Saxons| . Old Saxony corresponds roughly to the modern German states of  |Lower_Saxony|Lower_Saxony| ,  |Saxony_Anhalt|Saxony_Anhalt| , and the Westphalian part of  |North_Rhine_Westphalia|North_Rhine_Westphalia| .
