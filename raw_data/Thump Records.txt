     Thump Records is a  |record_label|Record_Label|  specialized in various genres of music. Founded by Bill Walker and  |Al_Lopez|Al_Lopez|  for $10,000. In 1997 Bill Walker became its sole C.E.O. and President. Thumps success was ignited by the popularity of its Old School and Low Rider compilation series . While a majority of the labels releases contain repertoire licensed from major labels like  |Universal|Universal_Music_Group| ,  |Sony|Sony| , and  |Warner_Bros.|Warner_Bros.| , Thump has also signed many artists under its own banner.
