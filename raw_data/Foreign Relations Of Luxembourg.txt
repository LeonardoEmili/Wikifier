The  Grand Duchy of Luxembourg  has long been a prominent supporter of  |European_political_and_economic_integration|European_Integration| . In 1921, Luxembourg and  |Belgium|Belgium|  formed the  |Belgium_Luxembourg_Economic_Union|Belgium_Luxembourg_Economic_Union|  to create an inter-exchangeable  |currency|Currency|  and a common  |customs|Customs|  regime. Post-war, Luxembourg became a founding member state of the  |United_Nations|United_Nations| , and dropped its policy of neutraility to become a founding member state of  |NATO|Nato| . Luxembourg expanded its support for European integration, becoming a founding member state of the  |Benelux_Economic_Union|Benelux_Economic_Union|  , and one of the  |inner_six|Inner_Six|  founding member states of the three  |European_Communities|European_Communities| ; the  |European_Coal_and_Steel_Community|European_Coal_And_Steel_Community|  , the  |European_Atomic_Energy_Community|European_Atomic_Energy_Community|  , and the  |European_Economic_Community|European_Economic_Community|  . Subsequently, Luxembourg became a founding member state of the  |European_Union|European_Union|  when the EEC and ECSC were incorporated into it in 1993. Luxembourg is a founding member of the  |Schengen_Area|Schengen_Area| , abolishing internal borders amongst its member states, named after  |the_Luxembourg_village|Schengen_Luxembourg|  where the  |original_agreement|Schengen_Agreement|  — since incorporated into  |EU_law|Eu_Law|  — was signed in 1985. At the same time, the majority of Luxembourgers have consistently believed that European unity makes sense only in the context of a dynamic transatlantic relationship, and thus have traditionally pursued a pro-NATO, pro-US foreign policy.     Luxembourg is the site of the  |European_Court_of_Justice|European_Court_Of_Justice| , the  |European_Investment_Bank|European_Investment_Bank| , the  |European_Court_of_Auditors|European_Court_Of_Auditors| , the  |secretariat_of_the_European_Parliament|Secretariat_Of_The_European_Parliament| , the Statistical Office of the European Commission , and other  |EU_bodies|Bodies_Of_The_European_Union| .
