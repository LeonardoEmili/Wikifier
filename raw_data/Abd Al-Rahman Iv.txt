Abd ar-Rahman IV Mortada  was the  |Caliph_of_Córdoba|Caliph_Of_Córdoba|  in the  |Umayyad|Umayyad|  dynasty in  |Al_Andalus|Al_Andalus| , succeeding  |Sulayman_ibn_al_Hakam|Sulayman_Ibn_Al_Hakam| , in 1018. That same year, he was murdered at  |Cadiz|Cadiz|  while fleeing from a battle in which he had been deserted by the very supporters which had brought him into power. His brief reign was similar to that of  |Abd_ar_Rahman_V_Mostadir|Abd_Ar_Rahman_V| .
