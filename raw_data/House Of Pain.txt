        House of Pain was an American  |hip_hop|Hip_Hop_Music|  trio who released three  |albums|Album|  in the 1990s before lead rapper  |Everlast|Everlast|  left to pursue his solo career. The groups name is a reference to the  |H.G._Wells|H.G._Wells|  novel  The Island of Dr. Moreau , a reference carried further by the naming of their 2011 tour He Who Breaks the Law. The group is best known for its 1992  |hit_single|Hit_Single| |Jump_Around|Jump_Around|  , which reached No.  3 in their native United States, No.  6 in Ireland and No.  8 in the United Kingdom. The group broke up in 1996 but re-formed in 2010, after the trio had been members of  |supergroup|Supergroup| |La_Coka_Nostra|La_Coka_Nostra|  for several years.          |Everlast|Everlast|  teamed up with  |DJ_Lethal|Dj_Lethal|  and high school friend  |Danny_Boy|Danny_Boy|  in Los Angeles to form House of Pain.  They attended  |Taft_High_School|William_Howard_Taft_Charter_High_School| . The group was signed to  |Tommy_Boy_Records|Tommy_Boy_Records|  and their 1992 debut album  Fine Malt Lyrics  went  |multi-platinum|Recording_Industry_Association_Of_America_Certification| , spawning the successful  |DJ_Muggs|Dj_Muggs| -produced single  |Jump_Around|Jump_Around|  . This song was also  |remix|Remix| ed twice by  |Pete_Rock|Pete_Rock| , one version featuring a verse from him and one without. The album also featured  |Cypress_Hill|Cypress_Hill|  member  |B-Real|B-Real|  on the song Put Your Head Out and  |Funkdoobiests|Funkdoobiest| |Son_Doobie|Son_Doobie|  on House and the Rising Son, both members of the musical collective known as  |Soul_Assassins|Soul_Assassins| .     Fashioning themselves as rowdy  |Irish-American|Irish_Americans|  hooligans , they toured with various rap and alternative-rock bands after their breakthrough. They participated together with  |Helmet|Helmet| , along with several other  |rap|Hip_Hop_Music|  acts, on the 1993  |rap_rock|Rap_Rock|  collaborative  Judgment Night  film  |soundtrack|Judgment_Night| .     Same As It Ever Was  Same as It Ever Was , went gold despite minimal airplay and no major hits. The first single, On Point , is noted for taking a swipe at another American rapper who claimed Irish heritage, Marky Mark  .     Truth Crushed to Earth Shall Rise Again  Truth Crushed to Earth Shall Rise Again , which featured guest appearances by rappers  |Sadat_X|Sadat_X|  of  |Brand_Nubian|Brand_Nubian| ,  |Guru|Guru|  of  |Gang_Starr|Gang_Starr| ,  |Divine_Styler|Divine_Styler|  and  |Cokni_ODire|Cokni_Odire|  of the Scheme Team. On the release date of the album, Everlast announced his departure from the group.       From then on, the members continued their separate careers. Danny Boy founded an art company. DJ Lethal became a member of  |nu_metal|Nu_Metal|  band  |Limp_Bizkit|Limp_Bizkit| , who would  |cover|Cover_Version|  Jump Around at live concerts, particularly in Limp Bizkits early years during the  |Family_Values_Tour_1998|Family_Values_Tour_1998| . Everlast achieved multi-platinum solo fame in 1998 with his album  Whitey Ford Sings the Blues . The first single from that album was  |What_Its_Like|What_Its_Like|  . In 2000, a feud between Everlast and rapper  |Eminem|Eminem|  coincided with the gold-selling  Eat at Whiteys , which included minor hits  |Black_Jesus|Black_Jesus|  and Black Coffee , and featured a collaboration with  |Carlos_Santana|Carlos_Santana| . After the sale of the Tommy Boy Records master tapes to  |Warner_Bros._Records|Warner_Bros._Records| , Everlast signed with  |Island/Def_Jam|The_Island_Def_Jam_Music_Group| , and released his  White Trash Beautiful  LP in 2004.     Later the same year  |Rhino_Records|Rhino_Entertainment| , a subdivision of Warner Music, released a hit collection,  Shamrocks & Shenanigans , with singles from Everlasts early solo days, the House of Pain and his post-group solo efforts. Before the release, Everlast announced on his official message board that he was not endorsing the  |compilation_album|Compilation_Album| .     Founded by Danny Boy in early 2006,  |La_Coka_Nostra|La_Coka_Nostra|  reunited him, Everlast and DJ Lethal for the first time since House of Pains split. Other group members include  |Ill_Bill|Ill_Bill|  of  |Non_Phixion|Non_Phixion| , and newcomer  |Slaine|Slaine| .       House of Pain reunited at a private event held by  |UFC|Ultimate_Fighting_Championship|  president  |Dana_White|Dana_White|  in  |Boston|Boston|  on  |St._Patricks_Day|Saint_Patricks_Day|  2009. It was officially announced on August 10, 2010, that House of Pain had reunited and performed their first official show in a decade at the second annual  |Epicenter_music_festiva|Epicenter|  l in  |Fontana,_California|Fontana,_California| , on September 25, 2010. Though Lethal is still a member of the group, he did not join them on their 2011 reunion tour due to prior obligations with  |Limp_Bizkit|Limp_Bizkit| .   In April–May 2011 House of Pain attended the  |Groovin_the_Moo|Groovin_The_Moo|  touring festival in Australia, and also performed at Scotlands  |T_in_the_Park|T_In_The_Park|  festival on July 9, 2011, as well as  |Sonisphere_UK_Festival|Sonisphere_Festivaluk|  on July 12 of the same year.       House of Pain reunited in 2017 for a 25th Anniversary Tour. The tour included shows in DC, Ohio, Pennsylvania, New York, Massachusetts, and California.
