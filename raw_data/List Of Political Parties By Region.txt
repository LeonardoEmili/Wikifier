     This page is a directory to various pages that will list  |political_parties|Political_Party| , from around the world, according to their respective regions. All of the pages linked from here include a table listing the sub-pages of countries/jurisdiction in the given region, showing which party system is dominant in each country.     A  |political_party|Political_Party|  is a  |political|Politics|  organization subscribing to a certain  |ideology|Ideology|  or formed around very special issues with the aim to participate in power, usually by participating in  |elections|Election| . Individual parties are properly listed in separate articles under the page for each nation.
