NOTOC   Bertrand Andrieu was a  |French|French_People| |engraver|Engraving|  of medals. He was born in  |Bordeaux|Bordeaux| . In  |France|Kingdom_Of_France| , he was considered as the restorer of the art, which had declined after the time of  |Louis_XIV|Louis_Xiv_Of_France| . During the last twenty years of his life, the French government commissioned him to undertake every major work of importance.
