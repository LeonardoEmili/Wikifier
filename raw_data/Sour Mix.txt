Sour mix  is a  |mixer|Drink_Mixer|  that is yellow-green in color and is used in many  |cocktails|Cocktail| . It is made from approximately equal parts  |lemon|Lemon_Juice|  and/or  |lime|Lime_Juice|  juice and  |simple_syrup|Syrupsimplesyrup|  and shaken vigorously with ice. This produces a pearly-white liquid with a pronounced flavor.     Optionally,  |egg_whites|Egg_White|  may be added to make the liquid slightly foamy.     Sour mix can be mixed with  |liquor|Liquor|  to make a  |sour_drink|Sour| ; most common are  |vodka_sour|Vodka_Sour|   and  |whiskey_sour|Whiskey_Sour|  .     Pre-mixed versions are available, and are in use in many bars. These typically consist of a powder which must be rehydrated by adding water prior to use.
