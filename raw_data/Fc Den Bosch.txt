FC Den Bosch  is a  |football|Association_Football|  club from  |s-Hertogenbosch|S-Hertogenbosch| , Netherlands.     They were founded 18 August 1965, as FC Den Bosch/BVV. They are the successor of BVV and Wilhelmina . Their stadium is called  |De_Vliert|De_Vliert| , an 8,500 all-seater.  |Ruud_van_Nistelrooy|Ruud_Van_Nistelrooy|  started his professional career at this club. In 2005 they finished bottom of the Eredivisie and were relegated.
