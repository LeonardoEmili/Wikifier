A magical organization or magical order is an organization created for the practice of  |magic|Magic|  or to further the knowledge of magic among its members. Magic in this case refers to  |occult|Occult| ,  |metaphysical|Metaphysics|  and  |paranormal|Paranormal|  activities, not to the performance of  |stage_magic|Stage_Magic| . Magical organizations can include  |hermetic|Ceremonial_Magic|  orders,  |Wicca|Wicca| n  |covens|Coven|  or Wiccan circles, esoteric societies, arcane colleges,  |witch|Witch| es covens, and other groups which may use different terminology and similar though diverse practices.
