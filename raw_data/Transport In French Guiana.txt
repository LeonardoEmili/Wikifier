The  |bus|Buses|  of the Joint Association of Public Transport serve only the municipality of  |Cayenne|Cayenne| . There are seven lines. Some lines have 3 or 4 buses. The price of  |ticket|T_Ticket| , the normal rate is 1.10 euro for 2011. Note that the SMTC is a  |public_body|Public_Body| , and the lines are restricted. SMTC buses now have a system of  |air_conditioning|Air_Conditioning| . The seats are more comfortable. These buses are also used to transport students from different schools. During the school year, the pregnant woman and senior citizens need to manage to find a place because these buses are always full, especially on market days. Buses only stop at bus stops . The wait time is about 15 to 40 minutes depending on the lines. Canoes are available however, fewer night and the prices are somewhat higher .          A short railway is used within the  |Guiana_Space_Centre|Guiana_Space_Centre|  . The railway is double tracked and used by unpowered rail cars and are towed by rubber wheeled vehicles with railway wheels or bogies to ride along the rail tracks.      From 1880s to sometime after 1926 a steam narrow gauge railway was used for gold mines in Saint-Elie  and two other lines were partially built and never used.      Prison railways were built in the 1890s but the line was abandoned after prisons closed and disappeared sometime after 1946.
