     Source tracking pertains to the ability of some  |hypertext|Hypertext|  systems to rigorously track the exact source of every document or partial document included in the system; that is, they remember who entered the information, when it was entered, when it was updated and by whom, and so on. This allows determining the exact history of every document .     Present  |HTML|Html|  and  |HTTP|Http|  do not have this feature, but certain systems on the  |World_Wide_Web|World_Wide_Web|   may have limited versions of the capability.
