Riddarfjärden  is the easternmost  |bay|Headlands_And_Bays|  of  |Lake_Mälaren|Mälaren|  in central  |Stockholm|Stockholm| . Stockholm was founded in 1252 on an island in the stream where Lake Mälaren drains into the  |Baltic_Sea|Baltic_Sea|  ; today the island is called  |Stadsholmen|Stadsholmen|  and constitutes  |Stockholms_Old_Town|Gamla_Stan| .
