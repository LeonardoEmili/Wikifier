Simple End User Linux is an advocacy group that promotes  |Linux|Linux|  programs in education and science.   SEUL also hosts numerous free software projects and efforts, such as the  |WorldForge|Worldforge|  Projects website. The SEUL/Edu project seeks to further the use of  |Linux|Linux|  and  |open_source_software|Open_Source_Software|  in schools, and was one of the groups which laid the groundwork for the  |SchoolForge|Schoolforge|  project. The SEUL/Sci project focused on the use of  |Linux|Linux|  and  |Free_Software|Free_Software|  in research.     Other project of note connected to SEUL is  |Tor_|Tor| .
