A large technical system  is a system or network of enormous proportions or complexity. The study of LTSs is a subdiscipline of  |history_of_science_and_technology|History_Of_Science_And_Technology| .     The book  Rescuing Prometheus  by  |Thomas_P._Hughes|Thomas_P._Hughes|  documents the development of four such systems, including the  |Boston|Boston| |central_artery_tunnel|Big_Dig| .
