     Tiffani Amber Thiessen is an American actress. She is known for starring as  |Kelly_Kapowski|List_Of_Saved_By_The_Bell_Characterskelly_Kapowski|  on  |NBCs|Nbc|   Saved by the Bell  and as  |Valerie_Malone|List_Of_Beverly_Hills,_90210_Charactersvalerie_Malone|  on  |Foxs|Fox_Broadcasting_Company|   Beverly Hills, 90210  . Thiessen has also starred in other TV series such as Foxs  Fastlane  ,  |ABCs|American_Broadcasting_Company|   What About Brian  , and  |USA_Networks|Usa_Network|   White Collar  , as well as in a number of TV movies, and she has also appeared in several films like  Son in Law  ,  Shriek If You Know What I Did Last Friday the 13th  ,  Hollywood Ending  , and  Cyborg Soldier  .       Thiessen was born in  |Long_Beach,_California|Long_Beach,_California| , the daughter of Robyn , a  |homemaker|Homemaker| , and Frank Thiessen, a park designer and  |landscape_architect|Landscape_Architect| .  She has credited her brother, Todd, as one of her most important sources of inspiration, and her mother and grandmother as role models.  Her mother has Greek, Turkish and Welsh heritage.  She attended Cubberley Elementary and Marshall Junior High School in Long Beach. In 1992, Thiessen graduated as  |valedictorian|Valedictorian|  at Valley Professional High School in  |Studio_City,_Los_Angeles|Studio_City,_Los_Angeles| .       Thiessen participated in beauty pageants during her childhood and was crowned Miss Junior America in 1987. The following year, she appeared on the cover of  Teen  magazine, having won the magazines Great Model Search . In 1989, she was named Cover Girl magazines Model of the Year , and landed her first role of  |Kelly_Kapowski|List_Of_Saved_By_The_Bell_Characterskelly_Kapowski-Morris|  on  |NBCs|Nbc|  television sitcom  Saved by the Bell , starring until its end in 1993. The success of the show gave her career a boost and Thiessen received  |Young_Artist_Award|Young_Artist_Award|  nominations for Outstanding Young Ensemble Cast in 1990 and Best Young Actress in an Off-Primetime Series in 1992 and 1993. Simultaneously, Thiessen guest starred on series such as  Married... with Children ,  Charles in Charge  and  Step by Step . In 1992, she starred in her first television movies  Saved by the Bell: Hawaiian Style  and  A Killer Among Friends , making her film debut in  Son in Law  in 1993. She then continued with starring in spin-off  Saved by the Bell: The College Years  in its single season and in television movie  Saved by the Bell: Wedding in Las Vegas  , which marked the end of the original Saved by the Bell series.     In 1994, after her tenure on Saved by the Bell had ended, Thiessen landed the role of  |Valerie_Malone|List_Of_Beverly_Hills,_90210_Charactersvalerie_Malone|  in  |Foxs|Fox_Broadcasting_Company| |Aaron_Spelling|Aaron_Spelling| -produced hit teen-drama  Beverly Hills, 90210 , to fill the void after  |Shannen_Dohertys|Shannen_Doherty|  departure. She was the first actress to join directly the main cast of the show and portrayed Valerie until 1998, when she left in the early episodes of the ninth season of the show. While starring in 90210, Thiessen began to take on continuous dramatic leading roles in television films:  The Stranger Beside Me  and  She Fought Alone  in 1995,  Sweet Dreams  and  Buried Secrets  in 1996. After her departure from the show, she appeared in a number of films, including  Speedway Junky  ,  From Dusk Till Dawn 2: Texas Blood Money  ,  Love Stinks  and  The Ladies Man  ; she also guest-starred on  NewsRadio  ,  Two Guys and a Girl  ,  Just Shoot Me!  and  Good Morning, Miami  . In 2000, she was featured in the  |music_video|Music_Video|  for  |Youre_a_God|Youre_A_God|  by  |Vertical_Horizon|Vertical_Horizon| , portraying a beauty pageant contestant, in her first music video appearance. That year she also dropped her middle name which she had been including professionally for many years and is now credited as simply Tiffani Thiessen.  Thiessen was Spellings first choice to replace Doherty in the series  Charmed  in 2001, but she refused his offer because she was hoping the NBC pilot for Everything But the Girl would be picked up.  The role eventually went to  |Rose_McGowan|Rose_Mcgowan| .     Meanwhile, Thiessen starred in direct-to-video parody film  Shriek If You Know What I Did Last Friday the Thirteenth  and in television movie Everything But the Girl , before appearing in the  |Woody_Allen|Woody_Allen| -directed film  Hollywood Ending  and starring in action drama series  Fastlane  as Wilhelmina Billie Chambers, for which she received a  |Teen_Choice_Award|Teen_Choice_Awards|  nomination for Choice TV Actress in Drama/Action Adventure. In 2003, she launched her production company Tit 4 Tat Productions, producing and directing the short film Just Pray in 2005,  which earned distinctions at several film festivals, including winning Best Score at the Rhode Island International Film Festival, an Academy Award-qualifying festival. The original motion picture soundtrack album, co-produced by Thiessen and Al Gomes of Big Noise, was placed on the Official Ballot for the 49th Annual Grammy Awards by The Recording Academy in several categories including Best Compilation Soundtrack Album for Motion Picture, Television or Other Visual Media.      Later on, she starred in television movies Stroller Wars and  Pandemic  , she joined the cast of  |ABCs|American_Broadcasting_Company|  drama  What About Brian  for its last five episodes and starred in the science fiction film  Cyborg Soldier  . In 2008, she made her second music video appearance, this time for  |Ben_Lees|Ben_Lee|  American Television . Thiessen appeared with her husband  |Brady_Smith|Brady_Smith|  on the September 6, 2009 episode of season four of the reality show  HGTV Design Star . Interior designer Lonni Paul, one of the final three contestants of the season, was given the task of redesigning Thiessens guest bedroom with an eco-friendly theme; Thiessen and Paul now have a nursery furniture line called PetitNest.  From 2009 through 2014 Thiessen appeared in the  |USA_Network|Usa_Network|  crime series  White Collar  as  |Elizabeth_Burke|List_Of_White_Collar_Characterselizabeth_Burke| .     On February 4, 2015, Thiessen reunited with  |Mark-Paul_Gosselaar|Mark-Paul_Gosselaar| ,  |Elizabeth_Berkley|Elizabeth_Berkley| ,  |Dennis_Haskins|Dennis_Haskins|  and  |Mario_Lopez|Mario_Lopez|  on  The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon  where they appeared in a Saved by the Bell sketch with Fallon.        Also in 2015, Thiessen started hosting her own show on  |Cooking_Channel|Cooking_Channel|  called  Dinner at Tiffanis . The series ended in 2017.     On June 7, 2017, Thiessen was cast as Lori Mendoza, mother of the Mendoza family on the multi camera  |Netflix|Netflix|  sitcom  Alexa & Katie , which premiered its first two seasons on March 23 and December 26, 2018.        In early 1992, Thiessen started dating  |Brian_Austin_Green|Brian_Austin_Green| , introduced by mutual friend  |David_Faustino|David_Faustino| ;  they moved in together in July 1994, then broke up the next year.  In 1999, she went through a difficult period in her personal life, when  |David_Strickland|David_Strickland| , also an actor, whom she had begun dating, died by suicide.  From 2001 to the spring of 2003, she was engaged to actor  |Richard_Ruccolo|Richard_Ruccolo| , whom she had met during the  Two Guys and a Girl  run.  She married actor  |Brady_Smith|Brady_Smith|  on July 9, 2005, and they have two children Harper Renn Smith, their daughter born in June 2010,  and Holt Fisher Smith, their son born in July 2015.      Thiessen served as  |maid_of_honor|Maid_Of_Honor|  at 90210 co-star and best friend  |Jennie_Garths|Jennie_Garth|  2001 wedding to actor  |Peter_Facinelli|Peter_Facinelli| , with whom she co-starred on  Fastlane . Garth and Thiessens friendship has since come to an end.  Her friendship with fellow 90210 star  |Tori_Spelling|Tori_Spelling|  also broke up, after Spellings split from her first husband.      In 2013, she participated in the One Bag party for April for Earth Month, in which the  |Glad|Glad|  company promoted clean,  |green|Environmentally_Friendly|  living .        Thiessen was a  |teen_idol|Teen_Idol|  during the 1990s, starting from her role as cheerleader  |Kelly_Kapowski|List_Of_Saved_By_The_Bell_Characterskelly_Kapowski|  on  Saved by the Bell  and then playing bad girl  |Valerie_Malone|List_Of_Beverly_Hills,_90210_Charactersvalerie_Malone|  on  Beverly Hills, 90210 . In a much-discussed moment,  |Justin_Bieber|Justin_Bieber|  appeared wearing a T-shirt with a photo of Thiessen in her Saved by the Bell days at the  |2011_MuchMusic_Video_Awards|2011_Muchmusic_Video_Awards| ,  admitting his admiration for her. Basketball player  |Chris_Paul|Chris_Paul|  also revealed publicly that he was a fan of Thiessen back then, when she sent him an autographed photograph of her Saved by the Bell-era for his 29th birthday in 2014.
