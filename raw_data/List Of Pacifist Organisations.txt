A Pacifist organization promotes the  |pacifist|Pacifism|  principle of renouncing war and violence for political ends. They are distinguished from organizations concerned only with removing  |nuclear_weapons|Nuclear_Weapon|  from war, though those organization may call for suspension of hostilities as well. Other organizations include those that deal with other concerns, but have a strong pacifist element.
