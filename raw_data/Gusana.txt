      Gusana is the name of an artificial  |lake|Lake|  and of the surrounding area, in the territory of  |Gavoi|Gavoi| ,  |Sardinia|Sardinia| ,  |Italy|Italy| .     The lake was built in the 1930s to store water for an electricity generator , and it covered an ancient  |Roman_bridge|Roman_Bridge|  as well as an ancient archaeological site of the  |Nuragici_people|Nuragic_Civilization| .     It is now a  |tourist_destination|Tourist_Destination| .
