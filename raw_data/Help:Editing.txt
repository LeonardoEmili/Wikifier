|Wikipedia|Wikipedia|  is a  |wiki|Wiki| , meaning anyone can edit any  |unprotected_page|Wikipedia:Protection_Policy|  and improve articles immediately. You do not need to register to do this, and anyone who has edited is known as a  |Wikipedian|Wikipedia:_Wikipedian|  or editor. Small edits add up, and every editor can be proud to have made Wikipedia better for all. There are two editing interfaces: the new  |VisualEditor|Wikipedia:Visualeditor|  and classic  |wiki_markup|Help:Wiki_Markup|  editing .     Some pages are  |protected_from_editing|Wp:Protection_Policy|  and marked by a lock icon at top right. If you are not allowed to edit the page, it will have a View  source instead of an Edit tab. You can still edit these pages indirectly by submitting an  |edit_request|Wikipedia:Edit_Requests|             An  |encyclopedic_style|Wikipedia:Encyclopedic_Style|  with a  |formal_tone|Wp:Tone|  is important: straightforward,  |just-the-facts|Wikipedia:Summary_Style| , instead of essay-like, argumentative, or opinionated. The goal of a Wikipedia article is to create a comprehensive and  |neutrally_written|Wp:Npov|  summary of existing mainstream knowledge about a topic. Wikipedia does not publish  |original_research|Wp:Nor| . An encyclopedia is, by its nature, a  |tertiary_source|Tertiary_Source|  that provides a survey of information already published in the wider world. Ideally, all information should be  |cited|Wikipedia:Citing_Sources|  and  |verifiable|Wp:Verify|  by  |reliable_sources|Wikipedia:Identifying_Reliable_Sources| . Sourcing requirements are significantly stricter in  |articles_on_living_persons|Wp:Blp| .      Edit screen      Editing most Wikipedia pages is simple. Wikipedia uses two interface methods: classic editing through  |wiki_markup|Help:Wiki_Markup|  ,and a new  |VisualEditor|Wikipedia:Visualeditor|  . Wiki markup editing is chosen by clicking the Edit tab at the top of a Wikipedia page . This will take you to a new page containing the editable contents of the current page. Wiki markup is used extensively throughout Wikipedia for such things as  |hyperlinks|Help:Link| ,  |tables|Help:Table|  and  |columns|Help:Columns| ,  .
