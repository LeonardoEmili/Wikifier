               Kurt Donald Cobain was an American singer, songwriter, and musician, best known as the guitarist and frontman of the  |rock|Rock_Music|  band  |Nirvana|Nirvana| . He is remembered as one of the most iconic and influential rock musicians in the history of  |alternative_music|Alternative_Music| .     Born in  |Aberdeen,_Washington|Aberdeen,_Washington| , Cobain formed the band Nirvana with  |Krist_Novoselic|Krist_Novoselic|  and  |Aaron_Burckhard|Aaron_Burckhard|  in 1987 and established it as part of the  |Seattle_music_scene|Music_Of_Seattle|  which later became known as  |grunge|Grunge| . After signing with major label  |DGC_Records|Dgc_Records| , Nirvana found success with  |Smells_Like_Teen_Spirit|Smells_Like_Teen_Spirit|  from their second album  Nevermind  . Following the success of Nevermind, Nirvana was labelled the flagship band of  |Generation_X|Generation_X| , and Cobain was hailed as the spokesman of a generation ;  however, Cobain resented this, believing his message and artistic vision had been misinterpreted by the public, with his personal problems often subject to media attention.      During the last years of his life, Cobain struggled with  |heroin|Heroin|  addiction and chronic health problems such as  |depression|Clinical_Depression| . He also struggled with the personal and professional pressures of fame, and his marriage to musician  |Courtney_Love|Courtney_Love| .  On April 8, 1994, Cobain was  |found_dead|Suicide_Of_Kurt_Cobain|  at his home in  |Seattle|Seattle|  at the  |age_of_27|27_Club| ; police concluded he had died on April 5 from a self-inflicted shotgun wound to his head.      Cobain has been described as a Generation X icon .  He was posthumously inducted into the  |Rock_and_Roll_Hall_of_Fame|Rock_And_Roll_Hall_Of_Fame| , along with Nirvana bandmates  |Dave_Grohl|Dave_Grohl|  and Novoselic, in their first year of eligibility in 2014.
