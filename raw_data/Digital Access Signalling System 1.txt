     Digital Access Signalling System 1  is a  |proprietary_protocol|Proprietary_Protocol|  defined by  |British_Telecom|British_Telecom|  to provide  |ISDN|Integrated_Services_Digital_Network|  services in the  |United_Kingdom|United_Kingdom| . It is now obsolete, having been replaced by  |DASS2|Dass2| . This too will become obsolete over the coming years as  |Q_931|Q_931| , a  |Europe|Europe| an standard, becomes widely adopted in the  |EU|European_Union| .
