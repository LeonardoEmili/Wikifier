|Réunion|Réunion| , is an  |overseas_département|Overseas_Département|  of France.     The islands conventional name is the Department of Réunion, or Réunion. It is also locally referred to as Île de la Réunion. The French flag is used on the island. The islands capital is  |Saint-Denis|Saint-Denis,_Réunion| , and the island is divided into four  |arrondissements|Arrondissement| , 24  |communes|Communes_Of_France|  and 47  |cantons|Cantons_Of_France| .     Réunion is governed by  |French_law|French_Law| , and its constitution is the  |French_constitution|French_Constitution|  of 28 September 1958. Suffrage is granted universally to all those over the age of 18.     The national holiday of the island is 14 July,  |Bastille_Day|Bastille_Day| , commemorating the  Fête de la Fédération  in 1790. The islands data code is RE .       The chief of state is the  |French_President|French_President| ,  |Emmanuel_Macron|Emmanuel_Macron| , who is represented by  |Prefect|Colonial_And_Departmental_Heads_Of_Réunion| |Jean-Luc_Marx|Jean-Luc_Marx| . The president of the General Council, who acts as head of the government, is  |Nassimah_Dindar|Nassimah_Dindar| .      |Elections|Elections|  held in Réunion include the  |French_presidential_vote|Elections_In_France| . A  |prefect|Prefectmodernsub-Nationaladministration|  is appointed by the president on the advice of the  |French_Ministry_of_the_Interior|French_Ministry_Of_The_Interior| . The presidents of the General and Regional Councils are elected by members of those councils.       The  |unicameral|Unicameral|  General Council has 47 seats. The unicameral Regional Council has 45 seats. Members of both are elected by  |direct_election|Direct_Election|  for six-year terms.                 Réunion elects seven deputies to the  |National_Assembly|National_Assembly|  for five-year terms.          Réunion elects four senators to the  |Senate|Senate|  for six-year terms.          Réunion participates in French elections to the  |European_Parliament|European_Parliament|  as a part of the Indian Ocean section of the  |Overseas_constituency|Overseas_Territories_Of_France| , which also includes  |Mayotte|Mayotte| .  |Younous_Omarjee|Younous_Omarjee| , from Réunion, is one of the three  |MEPs|Member_Of_The_European_Parliament|  representing the constituency.
